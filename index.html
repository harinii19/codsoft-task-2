About Us

Welcome to xyz Groceries, your number one source for all things fresh and local. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of groceries, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.
Founded in 2010 by Emma, xyz Groceries has come a long way from its beginnings in a small market stall in Portland. When Emma first started out, her passion for providing the best products for the community drove her to do intense research and quit her day job, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming grocery store.
We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
Sincerely,
Emma, Founder
